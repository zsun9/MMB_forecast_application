Summary of paper:
By constructing the model that accounts for micro data to quantify key features of US firm financing (COMPUSTAT), as well as credit spread and traditional macro variables, this paper uncovers the key forces of financial sector driving the US economy in the Great Recession.

Summary of model:
Extended medium-sized DSGE model with micro data to estimate financial gap spread and traditional credit spread, Bayesian estimation
methods to evaluate the contribution of financial intermediation disturbances to the US business cycles and focus on the precrisis period
- Common shocks: 1. monetary policy 2. inflation 3. demand shock 4. Investment shock
- Model specific shocks: 1. spread shock 2. financial gap spread shock 

Specific estimation method:
Bayesian method
