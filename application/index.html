MMB Online Forecasting Platform

The platform presents real-time macroeconomic forecasts from estimated macroeconomic and Bayesian VAR models.

You can compare the forecasts graphically and numerically. You can also explore the underlying mechanism by plotting input variables and checking the details of each model.
